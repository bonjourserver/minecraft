Shop
There is a currency, and you start with $0. /balance shows you your balance. /balancetop shows the balance leaderboard/ Use /shop to open the main shop menu.



From here, you can click on a category and see items. Clicking left click on the item will bring up the purchase menu, or clicking right click will sell that item. Shift + Right Click will sell your whole stock! /sellall sells every item in your inventory. /sellall hand sells the item(s) in your hand.

Auctioning
The auction house uses the same currency as the shop. Use /ah to open the auction house. Here you can buy items put up for sale by other players, or if I make a limited item! Use /ah sell if you want to sell the item you are holding in your hand. For example: /ah sell 10000 would sell the item in your hand for $10000
